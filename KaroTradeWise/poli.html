1. Product Listing and Information:

When you list a product for sale, you must provide clear and accurate information. This includes uploading pictures of the product from all sides and providing all requested details.

2. Communication:

We value communication and transparency. Both buyers and sellers should stay in contact throughout the transaction process. This helps ensure a smooth experience for all parties involved.

3. Buyer Satisfaction:

When you make a purchase through our platform, we strive to ensure that you are satisfied with your purchase. If you have any issues or concerns, please reach out to us, and we will work to resolve them.

4. Transaction Fees:

For each successful transaction, a fee of 20% will be deducted. This fee covers transportation and site commission. Please keep this in mind when setting your prices.

5. Prohibited Items:

We do not allow the sale of hazardous, carcinogenic, or harmful products on our platform. Please refrain from listing such items.

6. Documented Transactions:

All transactions and communication on our platform are documented. This is to provide a record of your interactions and to ensure accountability.

7. Location Information:

When buying or selling, it's important to provide accurate location information. This helps in arranging pickups, deliveries, and ensures a smooth exchange process.

8. Service Area:

Currently, our platform only operates within Karachi. We do not facilitate transactions outside of this area.

9. Price Limit:

We do not facilitate the sale or purchase of products with a price exceeding 50,000 rupees.

10. Agreement to Proceed:

By proceeding with using our platform for buying or selling, you agree to adhere to all the terms and conditions outlined above.
